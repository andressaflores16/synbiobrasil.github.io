COMING SOON...
July 24th - August 1st 2021
Given the current COVID-19 crisis, for the first time, the Brazilian National Symposium on Synthetic Biology will be held virtually, through online streaming platforms. Previously known as the Brazilian Jamboree, the symposium aims to promote the interaction and networking between students, researchers, and professionals in the field of Synthetic Biology in the country.
