EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE

We are a network of enthusiasts, dreamers, scientists, entrepreneurs and leaders who believe in the potential of Brazilian synthetic biology to transform the academy and industry - being a pioneering force in the world.
We share the inspiration of using synthetic biology to build solutions to complex global challenges. Hence, we believe that the intersection between engineering principles and the power of national biodiversity and bioeconomics will be the gear that will revolutionize the brazilian industry and academia over the coming years.
TO CONNECT
is to accelerate innovation, linking ideas with tools to build reality
TO REVOLUTIONIZE
is to believe in our national power and to break paradigms
TO EMPOWER
is to democratize and encourage synthetic biology, inspiring new talents
to develop Brazilian technologies and resources at the frontiers of knowledge
