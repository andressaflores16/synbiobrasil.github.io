About Us!
We are a network of enthusiasts, dreamers, scientists, entrepreneurs and leaders who believe in the potential of Brazilian synthetic biology to transform the academy and industry - being a pioneering force in the world.
We share the inspiration of using synthetic biology to build solutions to complex global challenges. Hence, we believe that the intersection between engineering principles and the power of national biodivertisity and bioeconomics will be the gear that will revolutionize the Brazilian industry over the coming years.
